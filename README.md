# StyleSync 👗✨

**StyleSync** is a full-stack web application designed to digitize and manage your personal wardrobe. It organizes your clothes, accessories, and shoes while providing weather-based outfit generation and laundry tracking system.

### 🚀 Core Features
* **Smart Cataloging:** Upload and categorize items by type (tops, bottoms, outerwear), color, and occasion.
* **Cloudinary Integration:** Seamless image hosting and management for your wardrobe photos.
* **Weather-Ready:** Outfit suggestions filtered by weather data.
* **Outfit Generator:** Builds an ensemble based on a specific piece choice.
* **Laundry Tracker:** Manage "Clean" vs. "Dirty" status to know exactly what’s available to wear.
* **Full Ensemble Support:** Dedicated modules for **Accessories** and **Footwear**.

---

### 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Frontend:** React.js
* **Backend:** Node.js & Express
* **Database:** MongoDB
* **Image Hosting:** Cloudinary
---

### 📸 How It Works
1. **Upload:** Snap a photo of your garment; Cloudinary handles the storage.
2. **Tag:** Assign types and weather suitability.
3. **Generate:** Use the Outfit Generator to pick a look based on your choice or the forecast.
4. **Maintain:** Mark items as "In Laundry" after wearing to remove them from suggestion pools.
